The pride of australia

Homegrown Mattress Excellence

When you choose one of our mattresses, you're not just investing in your best sleep; you're strengthening the fabric of your community and the environment.

How? 

Australian-made products contribute to the growth of local businesses and help sustain jobs. And because they're manufactured under fair labour conditions, workers are treated fairly. 

Supporting local craftsmanship also helps preserve the legacy and reputation of Australian-made mattresses for future generations.
Additionally, locally sourced materials for Australian-made products mean fewer carbon emissions from transportation, reducing the environmental impact of your purchase.

The Unique Edge of Our Mattresses

Along with our sustainability practices, innovation sets our mattresses apart.

Memory foam

Conforms to your body's shape for tailored support and reduced pressure points.

Gel-Infused Foam

Memory foam infused with gel particles improves breathability and temperature regulation.

Copper-infused foam

Copper particles also help regulate sleep temperature and inhibit the growth of bacteria and odours for a cooler, cleaner sleep environment.

Latex

A responsive, supportive, and durable sleep surface.

Hourglass back support

Reinforced support in the middle of the mattress provides extra support and spinal alignment for the lumbar region, especially for back and side sleepers.

Zoned pocket coils

Targeted body support and joint pressure relief where your body needs it most.

Mattress technology is ever-evolving, and so are we. Because we’re an Australian company, we can easily change our technologies and designs to adapt to the competitive Australian bedding market.

About Australian-Made Mattresses

Why Choose an Australian-Made Mattress?

Choosing an Australian-made, locally sourced material mattress means you get quality craftsmanship, support the local economy and jobs, and promote sustainability.

What Sets Australian-Made Mattresses Apart From Imported Ones?

Australian-made mattresses stand out from imported ones due to their commitment to premium craftsmanship, locally sourced materials, and support for local economy and sustainability initiatives.

What Kind of Warranty Can I Expect With an Australian-Made Mattress?

You can typically expect a warranty ranging from 10 to 15 years on an Australian-made Mattress Resources Australia mattress, giving you long-term peace of mind and investment satisfaction.
