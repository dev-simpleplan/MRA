Hypnos

Mattress

Named after the Greek god of sleep, Hypnos mattresses are crafted to offer you blissful sleep that's nothing short of heavenly.

Even the Royal family agrees! Hypnos is the proud holder of a Royal Warrant from Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II. This honour signifies that Hypnos is the only company actively supplying mattresses and beds to all British Royal residences.

You deserve a good night's rest just as much as the Royal family, so get yourself a Hypnos mattress today!

Sleep Royally on a Hypnos Mattress

Hypnos is a family-run business born in the heart of Britain. For 120 years, they've been consistently producing exquisite mattresses synonymous with luxury and comfort. This commitment to quality has earned them a Royal seal of approval. 

Hypnos combines the time-honoured artistry of mattress-making with the insights of modern sleep science to produce the highest quality mattresses for the highest quality sleep.

You can benefit from a Hypnos mattress in your home or business, as we supply a range of these mattresses to the accommodation industry across Australia. 

And the icing on the cake? Hypnos is committed to reducing its environmental footprint through low-carbon, sustainable materials and manufacturing practices. 
In fact, Hypnos in the UK became the first carbon-neutral bedmaker in the world and recently won the Queen's Award for Enterprise for Sustainable Development! So, when you choose a Hypnos mattress, you're investing in your sleep quality as well as in the health of our planet.

Master Craftsmanship

Meets Modern Innovation

How can the innovative features of a Hypnos mattress improve your sleep?

Nine-zoned pocket springs

These coils provide different levels of support across various areas of the mattress, keeping your spine aligned while giving extra support to heavier body parts like your hips and lighter support to your head and feet. This results in a more comfortable, restorative sleep, reducing your chances of waking up with aches or stiffness.

Mini pocket springs

These work alongside larger pocket coils for additional support, contouring your body more precisely for enhanced comfort and reduced pressure points.

HG and Stamina Foams

Both foams act as responsive material making them breathable and supportive by tailoring to your body's movements to help align your spine, and alleviates pressure points, perfect for any sleeping position and restless sleepers. Both foams have an antimicrobial treatment which helps minimise bacteria.

Graphite memory foam

An Ultra Fresh treated foam that has been designed for its breathability and helps disperse heat away evenly from the surface. Ultra Fresh is a an antimicrobial treatment that helps prevent microbe growth, prevent Odour and prevent hygiene and longevity to the product.

Gel-infused memory foam

Incorporates gel beads that adapt to your body's natural thermal changes and dissipate heat to keep you cooler for a better night's sleep.

Gel-infused latex

Combining the resilience and support of latex with the cooling properties of gel. This material also reduces motion transfer between sleepers.

Visco Soy Memory Foam

Renowned for its eco-friendliness, durability, and natural resistance to allergens and dust mites.

Natural cashmere and natural wool

Adds a layer of softness and comfort. These materials are also natural thermoregulators with moisture-absorbing properties, keeping you warm in the winter and cool in the summer and your sleep environment fresh and dry.

About Australian-Made Mattresses

Why Choose an Australian-Made Mattress?

Choosing an Australian-made, locally sourced material mattress means you get quality craftsmanship, support the local economy and jobs, and promote sustainability.

What Sets Australian-Made Mattresses Apart From Imported Ones?

Australian-made mattresses stand out from imported ones due to their commitment to premium craftsmanship, locally sourced materials, and support for local economy and sustainability initiatives.

What Kind of Warranty Can I Expect With an Australian-Made Mattress?

You can typically expect a warranty ranging from 10 to 15 years on an Australian-made Mattress Resources Australia mattress, giving you long-term peace of mind and investment satisfaction.
