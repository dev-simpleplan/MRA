Our partners' mattresses boast an array of beneficial advanced technologies and materials.

Therapedic

Therapedic mattresses feature gel-infused memory foam and SensIce textiles that draw heat away from your body and dissipate it to help you stay cool during the night. This is important as heat can increase wakefulness, reduce sleep efficiency, and alter sleep stages.

Hypnos

Hypnos uses premium materials in their mattresses, like cashmere, wool, and silk. These are not only comfortable; they also absorb and release moisture into the air, keeping you warmer in winter and cooler in summer. Some products also feature a top panel with Virase fabric treatment, which reduces the presence of harmful microbes and viruses.

Tommy Bahama

Tommy Bahama mattresses feature five-zone and nine-zone mini pocket coils that provide precise levels of support for different areas of your body.  They keep your spine aligned and reduce pressure points for a more comfortable, restorative sleep with fewer chances of waking up with aches or stiffness!

How Do I Choose the Right Mattress?

To choose the right mattress, consider 

  • Your sleep position: Side sleepers generally benefit from a softer mattress to alleviate pressure on the hips and shoulders, while back and stomach sleepers can need a firmer mattress for better spinal alignment.
  • Mattress firmness: Do you find plush, medium, or firm mattresses more comfortable?
  • Health concerns: If you have allergies or back pain, look for mattresses with hypoallergenic materials or targeted support.
  • Bedroom space requirements: You need enough space around your mattress for movement and other furniture.

Reviews: Read what others say about a mattress's comfort, durability, and performance to help you make an informed decision.
